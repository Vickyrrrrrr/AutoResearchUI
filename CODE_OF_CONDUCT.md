# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Participants in this project are expected to:

- be respectful and direct
- focus on technical substance
- assume good faith
- avoid harassment, abuse, and personal attacks

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- insulting or demeaning language
- targeted harassment
- deliberate intimidation
- publishing private information without permission
- repeated bad-faith disruption of discussions

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or reject contributions
that violate these standards.
